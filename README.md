Starterscan is a tool developed for the analysis of metagenomic samples from dairy starter cultures. It combines open source softwares with custom-built databases of genes and bacterial species, to provide a fast overview of the content of a WGS sample, including the detection and quantification of bacterial species and genes relevant for dairy starter cultures.
